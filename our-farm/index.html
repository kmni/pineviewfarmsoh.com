Pine View Farms is a 200 acre horse farm that operates under two main facilities. The Broodmare Farm features 60 stalls in 3 separate barns with 40 stalls being heated. There are 3 12x18 foaling stalls equipped with 24 hour monitoring. There are numerous spacious paddocks and expansive pastures on the Broodmare Farm. The new state of the art Stallion Barn is fully equipped with the latest equipment in breeding technology. The Show Farm is home to the Training Barn which houses 25 12x12 heated stalls, round pen and an 80x100 heated indoor arena.

 

We are conviently located in northeast Ohio off of State Route 21. Contact Us for directions or to schedule a visit to see our horses.
