Located in northeast Ohio, Pine View Farms is owned by Mike and Rochelle Farina. Established in 2000 the Farina's goal was to raise some of the best pleasure horse prospects in the country.

 

Believing the key to any successful breeding program is in the mares, Mike and Rochelle have meticulously hand picked a band of World Class broodmares. The young stallion "Lazy Loper" was purchased in 2003 and has since gone on to be a two time Reserve Congress Champion. His crowning achievement was in 2005 when he was named World Champion in Jr. Western Pleasure. Lazy's first full crop of weanlings arrived in 2006. Lazy's first group of 2yr olds were shown in 2008 and Lazy became a Congress Champion sire and World Champion sire from his first crop to show. Lazy Loper get have continued to dominate the show arena and he is now a multiple World Champion, Congress and Honor Roll sire. In November of 2005 the opportunity to purchase the incomparable "Invitation Only" presented itself. Believing that only the best will do Mike and Rochelle were successful in the purchase of "Invitation Only". Invitation Only has been the #1 Leading Sire in AQHA for an astonishing 6 years in a row starting in 2005 and continuing through 2011.
